What is Udgam?

Udgam is the annual spiritual, social and cultural Event of Odisha. It aims in promoting culture of Odisha. It marks of absolute ecstasy, providing the bud ding artists a competing platform in diverse fields such as music, dance, theatre, literature, fine arts, quizzing and debating. Odisha is an avenue to be comforted from the routine life and to embrace the fun and frolic embedded with tantalizing professional performances from India and abroad along with an addressal to the social responsibility with its underlying social theme. It's the 15th july to 21st july when Puri, Odisha's most cherished traditions and is renowned for the varied experiences, sweet memories and exciting stories that will create a feel of unity among all people.
